# Google Form setup

Suggested form fields:

- `Tytuł utworu`
- `Wykonawca`
- `Imię / stolik`
- `Komentarz`

Keep it short. Guests should be able to submit a request in a few seconds.

Recommended settings:

- do not require Google login,
- do not collect email addresses unless you really need that,
- connect responses to Google Sheets,
- show a short confirmation after submit.

Example confirmation:

`Thanks, your request was sent to the DJ.`
